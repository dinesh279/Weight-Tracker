2. Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) Guide

BMR represents the base calorie baseline your body burns strictly at rest to perform natural life support operations (breathing, organs support). Use this value to regulate weight management limits: to lose fat, secure a healthy calorie deficit below your TDEE output, but stay safely around your BMR threshold to avoid lean tissue loss.

3. Visceral Organ Fat Scale Index

Unlike the fat under your skin, visceral fat builds deep inside the abdomen surrounding critical organs. The proxy scores align as follows:

• 1 – 9 (Healthy Baseline): Balanced internal distribution; optimal low-risk framework.

• 10 – 14 (High Risk Index): Elevated abdominal layout; clean insulin-sensitive diets can quickly resolve this area.

• 15 – 20 (Critical Risk Index): Heavy organ stress encapsulation layer; prioritize cardiovascular pacing and consistent caloric targets.
